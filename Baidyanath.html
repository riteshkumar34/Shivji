Baidyanath Temple

Historical Background

Baidyanath Dham, in Deoghar, Jharkhand, is a revered Hindu temple housing one of the twelve Jyotirlingas, believed to have been established by the demon king Ravana. Over time, it has drawn pilgrims with its rich history, architectural beauty, and spiritual significance, making it a cherished site of devotion and cultural heritage in India.
