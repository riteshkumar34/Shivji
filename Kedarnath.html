Kedarnath Temple

Historical Background

It is believed that Pandavas built the temple of Kedarnath and the present temple was established by Adi Shankaracharya, who restored the glory of the shrine in the 8th century A.D. The temple is said to be more than 1,200 years old and one among the 12 jyotirlingas in India..

BELIEFS
The main belief of the people associated with Kedarnath Temple revolves around their deep devotion to Lord Shiva, considering the temple as one of the holiest abodes of the deity where prayers and rituals can bring blessings, protection, and spiritual fulfillment
