Nageshwar Temple

Historical Background

Nageshwar Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas dedicated to Lord Shiva, holds a significant place in Hindu mythology. Legend has it that the temple was established to commemorate the victory of Lord Shiva over the demon Daruka. Over the centuries, it has attracted devotees seeking blessings and spiritual fulfillment. With its ancient origins, cultural significance, and divine aura, Nageshwar Temple remains a revered pilgrimage destination for worshippers of Lord Shiva.
